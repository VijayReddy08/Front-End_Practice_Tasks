Chennai Super Kings (CSK) :

                                 

CSK team was led by the iconic MS Dhoni for many seasons, but for the 2024 IPL season, MS Dhoni stepped down entirely, and Ruturaj Gaikwad was appointed the new captain. CSK is a powerhouse known for its consistent performance. Their home ground, the M. A. Chidambaram Stadium in Chennai , often erupts in a sea of yellow , their signature color. CSK holds the record for most IPL trophies with five , with their most recent win coming in 2023 .
